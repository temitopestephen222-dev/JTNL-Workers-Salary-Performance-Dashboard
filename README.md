## Overview
A two-page interactive HR salary dashboard built in Power BI,
analyzing salary distribution, departmental spend, staff
categorization and employment status across 10 employees at
Jikemi Toplink Nigeria Limited.

## Tools Used
- Power BI — Dashboard & Visualization
- Microsoft Excel — Data Cleaning & Preparation

## Dashboard Pages

### Page 1 — Salary Overview
Covers high-level KPIs and salary trends across the organization.

**Visuals:**
- Average Salary by Hired Date (Line Chart)
- Workers Salary — individual breakdown (Bar Chart)
- Salary by Staff Type — New vs Old Staff comparison
- Staff Type and Status — Active vs Inactive breakdown
- Salary Percentage by Department (Donut Chart)

**Key Findings:**
- Company average salary is 63K across 10 workers
- Frank Miller is the highest earner at 91K, Grace Lee
  the lowest at 45K
- New Staff earn more on average (65K) than Old Staff (61K)
- Engineering accounts for 36% of total salary spend,
  the largest share across all departments
- 2017 and 2020 recorded the highest average salaries
  at hire, both above 80K

### Page 2 — Staff & Salary Breakdown
Covers individual worker status, salary band analysis by
employment status, department and age group.

**Visuals:**
- Employee Activity Status by Worker (Table)
- Salary Band Distribution by Employment Status (Bar Chart)
- Salary Band Distribution Across Departments (100% Stacked Bar)
- Salary Band Distribution by Age Group (Clustered Column Chart)

**Key Findings:**
- 7 out of 10 workers are Active, 3 are Inactive
- All workers in the High salary band are Active —
  none are Inactive
- Both Inactive workers fall in the Low salary band,
  suggesting a possible link between lower compensation
  and higher attrition risk
- Engineering is 100% High salary band, Marketing is
  100% Mid salary band
- Finance and HR are both split 50/50 between High and Low
- Workers aged 30-40 show the most salary diversity
